Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Nada Zero is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. This policy explains what the app accesses, how it uses that data, and what it does not do.

No data collection

Nada Zero does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All audio is generated entirely on your device in real time.

No network access

Nada Zero does not make any network requests. The app functions fully offline. No data ever leaves your device.

No collection, no tracking

Nada Zero does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Use analytics or crash reporting services
  • Contain advertising
  • Transmit any data over the network
  • Require an account or sign-in
  • Access the microphone, camera, location, or any other device sensors

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date.

Contact

Questions or concerns: open an issue on GitHub.
